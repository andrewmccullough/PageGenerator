Although I am a 100 and have only had a short season to get to know everyone, I wanted to let you know how influential and vital you have been to me and this detachment. I know I speak for many GMC when I say that we saw the pride and leadership you all possess with poise. For someone like myself, who had no prior military knowledge, coming into my first year of ROTC was difficult and quite overwhelming. During those challenging times, I may have felt differently about the positive influence that the program would have on my life, but now I look back at this past year and I am thankful. I may want to forget the constant fear of figuring out whether the POC twenty feet in front of me was male or female and analyzing their face to match a name before it was too late; or fumbling over my words saying “good morning” when in reality it was 7 p.m. at night. Yet, I will remember how thankful I am that God put me in ROTC at Detachment 890 to have you all teach and guide me, as well as, thankful that you demanded nothing but excellence, not only from yourself as leaders, but also from your fellow peers, and from us GMC below. I have been in situations in my past where leaders over me were toxic. They did not care about the program or those participating. They lacked passion, became prideful and simply slacked off once they held a position of leadership, all with the excuse that it was their last year to just have fun. Their leadership, or lack of, set the tone for everyone else to follow and I watched as those with so much potential became apathetic and unmotivated. But not with you. Never once did I sense this arrogance. I saw selfless leaders who understood the importance of their role in our detachment, as well as, their role in fighting for our country. I saw leaders who deeply cared and displayed guidance as well as character that will shape the future of the U.S. Air Force and our world. Thank you for providing me with someone to look up to and aspire to be in the future. I know God has an amazing plan for your lives as you have already touched mine.
